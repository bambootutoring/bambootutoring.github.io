FAQs

Common questions answered.

What subjects do you offer tutoring in?

We offer expert tutoring in Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Maths. Lessons are tailored to the student’s age, ability, and exam board, with a strong focus on building confidence and understanding. See the Services or Prices page for more.

What levels do you tutor?

We tutor students across a wide range of levels, including:

  • Primary (KS1 & KS2) - Maths and General Science
  • Secondary (KS3) - Maths and General Science
  • GCSE - Double and Triple Science, Maths
  • A-Level - Chemistry
  • Functional Skills - Maths (EL1-3 and L1-2)
  • Adult Learners / Foundation Support
What level is right for my child?

The right level depends on your child’s current year group, school curriculum, and learning goals. We assess each student individually and place them at the level that best supports progress—this may be above or below their school year if appropriate.

Do you offer Functional Skills tutoring?

Yes. Functional Skills tutoring is ideal for students who need practical maths for work, college, or life.

  • Level: Entry 1-3 / Level 1 / Level 2
  • Subject: Maths
Do you tutor adult learners?

Absolutely. We welcome adult learners returning to education or studying alongside work. Lessons are paced, supportive, and judgement-free.

How are lessons delivered?

Lessons are delivered Online via Google Meet. All sessions are interactive, structured, and tailored to the student.

How long is each lesson?

Typical lesson length is 60 minutes but can be flexible.

How do you personalise tutoring?

We personalise lessons by:

  • Identifying strengths and gaps
  • Adapting teaching style to the learner
  • Setting achievable goals
  • Regularly reviewing progress
Can you do group sessions?

Yes, fill out a contact form with information about subject and group numbers and then We will provide a quote.

How do I get started?

Simply get in touch with us via the contact us page. We’ll arrange a free consultation and create a personalised learning plan.
